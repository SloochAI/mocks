Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time. You'll continue to have Premium features until the end of your current billing period, then you'll be switched back to our free service.
How many songs can I download for offline listening? +
Premium subscribers can download up to 10,000 songs per device on up to 5 different devices. Downloaded songs will remain available for offline listening as long as you connect to the internet at least once every 30 days.
What's the difference between Premium and Family plans? +
Premium is for individual use with one account. Family includes all Premium features for up to 6 people living at the same address, with individual profiles and preferences for each family member.
Do you offer student discounts? +
Yes! Students can get Premium for just $4.99/month. You'll need to verify your student status through SheerID. The discount is available for up to 4 years and can be renewed annually.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely and you can change your payment method anytime.
